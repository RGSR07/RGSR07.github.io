What interests me most about Digital and Emerging Technology?

It was the summer of 2019, and that was the first time I experienced it up close and personal. One day, a friend of mine said she wanted to go to an exhibition, and I briefly browsed through it, and with the idea of wanting to dabble in some art at the time, I just bought a ticket. The exhibition was called Universe of Water Particles in the Tank. It is from Teamlab, a group of well-known Japanese "ultratechnologists", I learn from later. When I stepped through the door unprepared, I was completely blown away. I stood in the middle of the 270° digital "waterfall" and the flowers under my feet gradually bloomed, more and more, and before long they engulfed the waterfall. Then there was a gradual dissipation of the same frequency. The feast of light and shadow, full of technology, really opened my eyes. I strolled between the flowers and the waterfall, an immersive pleasure that even 4D movies could not let me experience.This exhibition was like a beginning for me and brought me into a whole new field. At the end of 2019, Teamlab opened a permanent art gallery in Shanghai, which I also visited once, and the lights there changed color wherever I stood, and if I saw a cluster of lights changing color in the distance, it meant that there was a person standing there. There was another one that made a deep impact on me, Ocean By Naked, where I swam with sharks at 6500m under the sea and listened to rainbow-colored whales murmuring at 423m. I used to be a person who was very afraid of the sea, but there I had the ability to create vortices with my bare hands. As a visitor, it was amazing, and I couldn't imagine how fast technology has evolved. What appeals to me most about Digital and Emerging Technology is that designers and engineers use cold codes and machines to create interesting and fascinating scenarios.
